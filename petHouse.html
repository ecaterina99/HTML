Adopt a dog

Adopting a dog from a shelter gives them a second chance at a happy life. Many of these dogs have faced hardships and are waiting for someone to care for them. By providing a loving home, you’re offering them the joy and security they deserve while making room in the shelter for another animal in need.

About Us

Give Love, Save Lives — Adopt, Volunteer, Support!

This animal shelter, located in Iași, Romania, has been providing care and hope for homeless pets since its opening in 2019. With over 400 animals currently under its protection, the shelter is dedicated to providing food, medical care, and a safe environment for those in need. Remarkably, it has already helped 1,250 pets find loving homes. The shelter’s mission is to care for and rehabilitate stray animals, giving them a chance at a better life.
