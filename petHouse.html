About Us

Give Love, Save Lives — Adopt, Volunteer, Support!

This animal shelter, located in Iași, Romania, has been providing care and hope for homeless pets since its opening in 2019. With over 400 animals currently under its protection, the shelter is dedicated to providing food, medical care, and a safe environment for those in need. Remarkably, it has already helped 1,250 pets find loving homes. The shelter’s mission is to care for and rehabilitate stray animals, giving them a chance at a better life.

Happy Tails

Many families have opened their hearts to shelter animals, giving them a chance at a better life. These pets, once homeless, now thrive in loving homes, bringing joy and companionship to their new families. The families are overjoyed to have made a difference, knowing they saved not just lives but also gained loyal friends for life.

Rex

When Rex first arrived at his new home, he timidly explored every corner, but within a week, his tail never stopped wagging. Now he’s the family’s protector, children’s best friend, and a loyal companion on every walk.

Pufarina

Tiny Pufurina, once scared and skinny, quickly blossomed into the fluffy queen of the house. Every morning, she greets her owners with gentle purring, a daily reminder of the magic of love.

Tom & Layla

Layla and Tom became friends at the shelter and were adopted together. Now inseparable, the dog guards the home while the cat warms the family with cozy purrs on the couch.

Reno

Reno joined a young couple and instantly became their best friend. His playful personality and funny jumps filled their home with laughter and joy.

Pupic

Little Pupic, rescued from the streets, transformed into a lively comedian in his new home. He brightens every day with joyful leaps on walks and his ability to turn anything into a game.

FAQs

To adopt a pet you see listed on Pet House, please click the "Adopt" button or the "Contact this Organization" button on the pet's profile. You will then be given the group's contact information and, if an email is available for that group, you will be able to email that group using our form.

Pet House Animal Society is funded mostly by the donations of members and well-wishers. We also receive some funding from foundations and businesses. Donations to Pet House are tax-deductible. Our accounts are fully audited.

Most of the animals come from Pet House Network partners, which are located all around the country.

The animal shelter Pet House is located in Iași, Romania. It has been providing care and hope for homeless pets since its opening in 2019.

We couldn't fund our life-saving work for animals without you. More than 80 percent of your donation will go to rescuing and caring for vulnerable animals, then rehoming or releasing them. The next 18 percent of your donation goes towards fundraising. The remaining 2 percent goes towards governance. Your generous donation helps us fight animal cruelty—thank you.
